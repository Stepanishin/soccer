NFT COLLECTIONS

Lazy Alpha (LA)

  • A limited alpha collection with a supply of only 1000 NFTs. The main benefits will be available for long-term holders of at least two Lazy Alpha NFTs that are held on the same wallet
  • Membership in the DAO and alpha chat with access to the most exciting projects of the Solana ecosystem at an early stage will be available for holders of at least two Lazy Alpha NFTs
  • Using Lazy Alpha NFT as a multiplier in the Lazy Soccer game. NFT holders will gain increased game rewards, which can directly affect the speed of achieving game results
  • After the game release, only the owners of two Lazy Alpha NFTs held on the same wallet will be able to mint the new Lazy Soccer Manager NFT required for the game access. The mint will require project tokens that will be burned
  • An airdrop of the Lazy Soccer Manager NFT and a possible airdrop of a certain number of Lazy Soccer project tokens to holders of two Lazy Alpha NFTs, held on wallets during each snapshot. Snapshots will be taken at random times without warning
  • Holders of the Lazy Alpha NFT will be able to receive an allocation in the token sale of the Lazy Soccer project

Lazy Soccer Manager (LSM)

  • A collection to access the Lazy Soccer game. In order to play, you will need either Lazy Soccer Manager Genesis NFT (original collection with a limited supply of 5000 NFTs) or Lazy Soccer Manager NFT (NFTs minted for tokens by Lazy Alpha NFT holders);
  • The Lazy Soccer Manager Genesis NFTs will work as a multiplier and provide small game benefits depending on the rarity. Lazy Soccer Manager NFTs minted by the holders of Lazy Alpha NFTs are not multipliers, but give the right to play the game;
  • Holders of the Lazy Soccer Manager Genesis NFTs will be able to receive an allocation in the token sale of the Lazy Soccer project;
  • A possible airdrops of the following collections for the Lazy Soccer Manager Genesis NFT holders.

FAQ

What is Lazy Soccer?

The main product of our project is the play2earn football manager Lazy Soccer. This is an intellectual game with a well-thought-out economic model in which you will manage a team of sloths. The game will require holding a Lazy Soccer Manager Genesis or Lazy Soccer Manager NFT on the wallet.

What platform will Lazy Scoccer be available on?

We are developing a multi-platform game that will be available on the desktop, as well as on iOS and Android.

What is Lazy Alpha collection?

This is the initial NFT collection of the project — our bonus for early adopters and thanks to the original community of the project for their support at an early stage. In total there will be 1000 Lazy Alpha NFTs at a price of 0.69 SOL.

The main purpose of the existence of the Lazy Alpha NFT before the Lazy Soccer game release is to create a DAO and an alpha chat, which will be available only for holders of the two Lazy Alpha NFTs.

In the future, NFTs from the Lazy Alpha collection will be used as multipliers in the game and will allow you to get more benefits in the Lazy Soccer play2earn game. In addition, each owner of the Lazy Alpha will be able to mint the Lazy Soccer Manager NFT using the project tokens.

Check other benefits in the Utility section.

What is Lazy Soccer Manager Genesis NFT collection?

This is an NFT collection of the soccer club sloths managers to be used to play the Lazy Soccer game. Holders of the Genesis Collection will receive a number of in-game benefits that will slightly increase the efficiency of upgrading the team and can increase the possible return on investment.

Check other benefits in the Utility section.

What is Lazy Soccer Manager NFT?

This is an NFT to access the game that Lazy Alpha NFT holders will be able to mint. The essence of this collection is similar to the essence of the Lazy Soccer Manager Genesis NFT, but there are no in-game benefits for this collection.

How to get a spot in the Lazy Alpha whitelist?

All active helpful members of the community who show a passion for the project will get a spot in the Lazy Alpha collection whitelist. Activity is encouraged, but we’re not going to look at the number of discord invites or your level on the server. It is important for us to create an engaged community passionate about our product.
